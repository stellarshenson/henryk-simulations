# Testimony - Victim

Victoria states she was pushed by Andrew against the elevator door, struck the door with her back, and fell to the floor striking her buttocks. She reports no other injuries and no loss of consciousness.

Examination findings:

- Bruising of the skin of the right shoulder extending onto the arm, approximately 12 cm × 6 cm
- Edema and moderate pain-limited range of motion in the right shoulder joint
- X-ray of the right shoulder: no bony injuries
- Forward flexion of the neck full and unrestricted; right-left rotation full with mild pain in the cervical spine
- Reported symptoms: shoulder pain radiating to the right arm and forearm with numbness of the fingers of the right hand, and headache
