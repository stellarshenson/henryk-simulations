# Mero, Komi & Gregor (1992) - Biomechanics of Sprint Running

Mero, A., Komi, P.V. & Gregor, R.J. (1992). Biomechanics of sprint running. *Sports Medicine*, 13(6), 376-392.

**Access**: Paywalled (Springer / Adis).

## What it establishes

Peak horizontal centre-of-mass acceleration in the first 0.2 s of a standing start, for recreational and elite male sprinters.

## Values used in this project

Recreational male peak CoM acceleration 3.0 m/s^2 (SD 0.8); elite sprinter 5.0 m/s^2 (SD 0.5).

## Where it is used

Exclusion-zone `a_max` ceiling (5.5 m/s^2 = elite mean + 1 SD) in notebook 01b; sprint-acceleration distribution in `references.py`.
