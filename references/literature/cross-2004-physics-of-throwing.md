# Cross (2004) - Physics of Overarm Throwing

Cross, R. (2004). Physics of overarm throwing. *American Journal of Physics*, 72(3), 305-312.

**Access**: Paywalled (American Association of Physics Teachers).

## What it establishes

Release-velocity and kinetic-energy budgets for hand-thrown objects ranging 0.15-5 kg.

## Values used in this project

5 kg object: release velocity about 8 m/s (SD 2.5), kinetic energy about 160 J (SD 80), interpolated from the population sample.

## Where it is used

Throw kinematics distribution in `references.py`.
