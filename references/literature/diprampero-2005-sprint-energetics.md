# di Prampero et al. (2005) - Sprint Running Energetics

di Prampero, P.E., Fusi, S., Sepulcri, L., Morin, J.B., Belli, A. & Antonutto, G. (2005). Sprint running: a new energetic approach. *Journal of Experimental Biology*, 208, 2809-2816.

**Access**: Open access (Journal of Experimental Biology, https://journals.biologists.com/jeb). No PDF copy held locally.

## What it establishes

An energy-based estimate of sprint acceleration that cross-checks the Mero kinematic figures.

## Values used in this project

Energy-based acceleration estimates consistent with the Mero 3-5 m/s^2 range.

## Where it is used

Cross-reference for the sprint-acceleration bound.
