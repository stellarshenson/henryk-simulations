# Viano (1989) - Biomechanical Responses and Injuries in Blunt Lateral Impact

Viano, D.C. (1989). Biomechanical responses and injuries in blunt lateral impact. In: *Proceedings of the 33rd Stapp Car Crash Conference*, SAE Technical Paper 892432. Society of Automotive Engineers. DOI: 10.4271/892432

**Access**: Paywalled (SAE Mobilus). PDF not held locally.

## What it establishes

Thoracic blunt-impact injury criteria on the AIS scale, from pendulum impacts on unembalmed cadavers. Relates compression and viscous response to injury severity.

## Values used in this project

Roughly 1.6 kN per loaded rib at AIS 2 or greater injury.

## Where it is used

The `fracture_thresholds()` rib-fracture-per-rib threshold in `corridor/impact.py`.
