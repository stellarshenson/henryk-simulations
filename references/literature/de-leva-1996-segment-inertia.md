# de Leva (1996) - Adjustments to Segment Inertia Parameters

de Leva, P. (1996). Adjustments to Zatsiorsky-Seluyanov's segment inertia parameters. *Journal of Biomechanics*, 29(9), 1223-1230. DOI: 10.1016/0021-9290(95)00178-6

**Access**: Paywalled (Elsevier). PDF not held locally.

## What it establishes

Adjusted body-segment inertia parameters - relative masses, centre-of-mass positions and radii of gyration - re-referenced from the original Zatsiorsky-Seluyanov bony landmarks to the joint centres used by most biomechanics researchers. Derived from a gamma-ray scan of college-aged male and female subjects.

## Values used in this project

Male segment mass fractions of total body mass: head 6.94%, thorax 15.96%, abdomen 16.33%, pelvis 11.17%, upper arm 2.71%, forearm 1.62%, hand 0.61%, thigh 14.16%, shank 4.33%, foot 1.37%.

## Where it is used

Notebook 02 body-segment model and the effective-impacting-mass calculation in `corridor/impact.py`.
