# Mital & Kumar (1995) - Human Muscle Strength

Mital, A. & Kumar, S. (1995). Human muscle strength definitions, measurement, and usage. *International Journal of Industrial Ergonomics*, 16(4), 237-256.

**Access**: Paywalled (Elsevier).

## What it establishes

Confirms the Daams push-force figures and adds a fatigue qualification for sustained exertion.

## Values used in this project

Sustained 30 s force is about 60% of the peak voluntary value.

## Where it is used

Corroborates the push-force distributions in `references.py`.
