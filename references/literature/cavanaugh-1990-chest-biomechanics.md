# Cavanaugh et al. (1990) - Thoracic Blunt-Impact Biomechanics

Cavanaugh, J.M., Walilko, T.J., Malhotra, A., Zhu, Y. & King, A.I. (1990). Biomechanical response and injury tolerance of the thorax in twelve sled side impacts. In: *Proceedings of the 34th Stapp Car Crash Conference*, SAE Technical Paper 902307, pp. 23-38. Society of Automotive Engineers.

**Access**: Paywalled (SAE Mobilus). PDF not held locally. DOI not confirmed.

## What it establishes

Chest and rib stiffness and AIS-graded thoracic injury tolerance from cadaver sled side impacts, including AIS 3 or greater thoracic injury at about 25 mm of chest deflection.

## Values used in this project

25 mm deflection AIS 3 or greater threshold; relaxed anterior chest stiffness baseline of about 50 N/mm.

## Where it is used

The `fracture_thresholds()` thoracic-deflection threshold in `corridor/impact.py`.

## Citation note

Cavanaugh's thoracic-tolerance work is sometimes cited under other years in injury-biomechanics shorthand; the verifiable source for the 25 mm AIS 3+ deflection and the chest-stiffness values is the 1990 sled side-impact study above.
