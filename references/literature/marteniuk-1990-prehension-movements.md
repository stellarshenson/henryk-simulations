# Marteniuk, MacKenzie & Leavitt (1990) - Prehension Movements

Marteniuk, R.G., MacKenzie, C.L. & Leavitt, J.L. (1990). Functional relationships between grasp and transport in prehension movements. *Human Movement Science*, 9(2), 149-176.

**Access**: Paywalled (Elsevier).

## What it establishes

Hand peak velocity during goal-directed reaching movements.

## Values used in this project

Hand peak velocity during a goal-directed reach 2.5 m/s, SD 0.8 m/s, for arm extension of 0.4-0.7 m.

## Where it is used

Reach-velocity distribution in `references.py`.
