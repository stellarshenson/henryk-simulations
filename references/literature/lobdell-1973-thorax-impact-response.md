# Lobdell et al. (1973) - Impact Response of the Human Thorax

Lobdell, T.E., Kroell, C.K., Schneider, D.C., Hering, W.E. & Nahum, A.M. (1973). Impact response of the human thorax. In: King, W.F. & Mertz, H.J. (eds), *Human Impact Response: Measurement and Simulation*, pp. 201-245. Plenum Press, New York. DOI: 10.1007/978-1-4757-1502-6_11

**Access**: Paywalled (Springer book chapter). PDF not held locally.

## What it establishes

The lumped mass-spring-damper model of the thorax under blunt impact. Reviews cadaver thoracic impact data and fits a multi-element mechanical model whose force-deflection corridors became dummy design guidelines.

## Values used in this project

The structure of the 5-DOF posterior-thorax chain - skin, scapula, ribcage, organ and spine masses linked by springs and dampers - follows the Lobdell lumped-parameter form.

## Where it is used

The `solve_impact` chain in `corridor/impact.py`.
