# Plagenhoef, Evans & Abdelnour (1983) - Anatomical Data

Plagenhoef, S., Evans, F.G. & Abdelnour, T. (1983). Anatomical data for analyzing human motion. *Research Quarterly for Exercise and Sport*, 54(2), 169-178.

**Access**: Paywalled (Taylor & Francis).

## What it establishes

Standard tabulation of body-segment masses and moments of inertia.

## Values used in this project

Whole-body yaw moment of inertia about the CoM for a 75 kg standing male: 1.5 kg m^2, SD 0.4.

## Where it is used

`victoria_yaw_inertia_kgm2` in notebook 01b - the assumed human-body yaw moment of inertia used by the Phase-2 Duration Constraint section; yaw-inertia distribution in `references.py`.
