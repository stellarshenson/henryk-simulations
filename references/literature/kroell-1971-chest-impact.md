# Kroell, Schneider & Nahum (1971) - Impact Tolerance and Response of the Human Thorax

Kroell, C.K., Schneider, D.C. & Nahum, A.M. (1971). Impact tolerance and response of the human thorax. In: *Proceedings of the 15th Stapp Car Crash Conference*, SAE Technical Paper 710851. Society of Automotive Engineers. DOI: 10.4271/710851

**Access**: Paywalled (SAE Mobilus). PDF not held locally.

## What it establishes

Cadaver chest force-deflection corridors for blunt sternal impact, with rib fracture progression at roughly 5-7 cm of chest compression. Tested unembalmed specimens of both sexes across a range of impact velocities.

## Values used in this project

The force-deflection context and the 5-7 cm chest-compression range used to bound the posterior-thorax model.

## Where it is used

Notebook 02 fracture-threshold context.
