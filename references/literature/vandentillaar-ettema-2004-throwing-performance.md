# van den Tillaar & Ettema (2004) - Overarm Throwing Performance

van den Tillaar, R. & Ettema, G. (2004). Effect of body size and gender in overarm throwing performance. *European Journal of Applied Physiology*, 91(4), 413-418.

**Access**: Paywalled (Springer).

## What it establishes

Between-subject variance estimates for overarm throwing performance.

## Values used in this project

Variance estimates feeding the throw-velocity SD.

## Where it is used

Variance source for the throw distribution in `references.py`.
