# Kemper et al. (2014) - Rear-Torso Blunt-Impact Biomechanics

Kemper, A.R. et al. Rear-torso impact biomechanics: dynamic response and injury tolerance of the posterior thorax. *Journal of Biomechanics* (Elsevier), article PII S0021929015003772. https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0021929015003772

**Access**: Paywalled (Elsevier). PDF not held locally. The article PII indicates a 2015 publication; the project reference list (`references/incident/impact_analysis.md` ref 14) cites it as Kemper et al. 2014 - the year should be reconciled against the publisher record.

## What it establishes

Posterior-thorax (rear-torso) blunt-impact tolerance from cadaver testing. Rear-torso impact loads of 6.9-10.5 kN produce rib fractures and costo-vertebral and costo-transverse joint injuries.

## Values used in this project

The 6.9-10.5 kN posterior-thorax tolerance band.

## Where it is used

The `fracture_thresholds()` posterior-thorax-tolerance threshold in `corridor/impact.py`.

Volume, issue, pages and DOI are not recorded here; the article is identified by its ScienceDirect PII. The 6.9-10.5 kN posterior-thorax tolerance band is the value the impact model takes from it.
