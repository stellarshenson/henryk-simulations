# Hodgson, Lewis & Drury (2008) - Turning While Walking

Hodgson, A.J., Lewis, J. & Drury, C.G. (2008). A turning-while-walking task with cognitive load. *Applied Ergonomics*, 39(3), 386-396.

**Access**: Paywalled (Elsevier).

## What it establishes

Peak yaw angular velocity during a voluntary 180 degree standing pivot.

## Values used in this project

Peak yaw angular velocity 3.5 rad/s, SD 1.0 rad/s.

## Where it is used

Phase-2 Duration Constraint section of notebook 01b - the 180 degree rotation yaw-rate floor (3.5 rad/s population, 5.5 rad/s elite) that locks the phase-2 duration; rotational exclusion axis; yaw-rate distribution in `references.py`.
