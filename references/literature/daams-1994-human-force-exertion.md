# Daams (1994) - Human Force Exertion in User-Product Interaction

Daams, B.J. (1994). *Human force exertion in user-product interaction.* Delft University Press, Delft.

**Access**: Book / doctoral thesis (Delft University Press). Not available as a downloadable PDF.

## What it establishes

Tabulates standing two-arm and single-arm push force for adult subjects across stances and postures.

## Values used in this project

Two-arm standing push, unloaded subject: mean 800 N, between-subject SD 200 N. Single-arm dominant: mean 400 N, SD 100 N.

## Where it is used

`src/henryk_simulations/corridor/references.py` push-force distributions; phase-2 push-off bound in notebook 01b.
