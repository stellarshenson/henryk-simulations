# Testimony - Third Party (Court Social Curator)

Cecilia stood in segment 1 of the corridor, overlooking segment 2 with a clear view of the apartment door area where Andrew and Victoria were positioned. Victoria stood at the apartment door (N wall), facing south toward Andrew. Andrew stood with his back to the elevator door (S wall), facing north toward Victoria. The apartment door and the elevator door face each other across the ~2 m corridor width of segment 2.

Victoria took a toy, said she would attract the child outside with it by placing it on the corridor floor, and asked Cecilia to step aside so the toy could roll into the corridor.

Cecilia took three steps and turned away briefly to clear the line for the toy.

When Cecilia turned back, Victoria was already leaning against Andrew, front-first. Andrew's hands were raised.

Victoria began to scream at the moment she saw that Cecilia was turned back and looking at them. Cecilia stated this explicitly: *"...and at that moment she started screaming, when she saw that I was already turned [back], she started screaming."*

Victoria then slid down a door surface, front-first, onto the floor and crawled on all fours.

Cecilia did not see the moment of contact itself. From segment 1 her line of sight to segment 2 was clear during the visit; she lost that view only during the brief turn-aside that Victoria had requested.
